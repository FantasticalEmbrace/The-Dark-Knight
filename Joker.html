Joker

Batman crept in the cover of the night after the police were all gone. Scouring through the barbarous scene before him, he sought any clues that would lead him to the assailant. His first and most mysterious clue was the playing cards scattered about the room. It was apparent that he was not dealing with the same class of criminal that would rob banks or innocents walking in alleyways for the sake of greed. No, this was a different creature that found humor in chaos. This fact was made evident by the grins drawn on the lifeless faces distributed throughout and that the playing cards left behind were the same type: the Joker.
